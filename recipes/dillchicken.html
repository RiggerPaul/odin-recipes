Creamy Dill & Chicken Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 (8 ounce) package baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • ¼ cup dry white wine
  • 1 tablespoon dry sherry
  • ½ cup plain low-fat Greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh dill, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

Directions

    Step 1

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.

    Step 2

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken and cook for 5 minutes.

    Step 3

  1. Reduce heat to medium and add onion; cook until translucent and soft, about 3 minutes.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, for 4 to 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

    Step 4

  1. Pour in chicken broth, wine, and sherry and bring to a boil.
  2. Stir in Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup dill, and Dijon mustard until well incorporated.
  3. Add butter and stir until melted.

    Step 5

  1. Place flour in a small bowl.
  2. Add a ladle of hot broth and whisk until smooth and no lumps remain.
  3. Pour mixture into the skillet and stir until well incorporated

    Step 6

  1. Reduce heat to low and simmer until the sauce starts to thicken, 3 to 4 minutes.
  2. Taste and adjust seasoning and/or add additional dill if necessary.

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 295 calories; fat 14.4g; cholesterol 111.9mg; sodium 227.7mg; carbohydrates 10.7g; protein 27.7g
